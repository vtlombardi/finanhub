# Product Rules & Logic

- **Controlled Communication**: No direct contact between buyers and sellers.
- **Deal Flow**: All negotiations must be mediated via the platform and Admin panel.
- **Lead Capture**: Compulsory lead form; entry point for any business inquiry.
- **Mediation**: Admin serves as the central handler for all pipelines.

## Credibility System
- **Verification**: "Verificado FH" status for screened assets (Due Diligence).
- **Market Score**: Rating system based on financial maturity and liquidity.
- **Privacy**: Internal notes and scoring are strictly for Admin use.

## Dashboard Logic
- **Investor**: Focused on visibility of assets, tracked deals, and communication.
- **Advertiser**: Focused on lead status visibility without internal Admin notes.
